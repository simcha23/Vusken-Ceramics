About Vusken Ceramics

Vusken Ceramics is a ceremic manufacturig company from the rural village by the name of Mthambothini under Dr Js Moroka Municipality. As a result of the gap which we identified in the area the company was established. We design and manufacture ceramic products and we have introduced the world renowned and unique Ndebele patterns in our products.

We have adopted innovative technologies from around the globe to further more set ourselves apart as we are collaborating our traditional patterns with technology. We use different techniques for our pieces so as to meet our clients specifications.
